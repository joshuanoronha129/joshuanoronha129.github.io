Speaker of the Month

Fossil Threads in the Web of Life

February

Scott Sampson

What’s 75 million years old and brand spanking new? A teenage Utahceratops! Come to the Saroyan, armed with your best dinosaur roar, when Scott Sampson, Research Curator at the Utah Museum of Natural History, steps at the podium. Sampson’s research has focused on the ecology and evolution for late Cretaceous dinosaurs, and he has conducted fieldwork in a number of countries in Africa.
